Welcome to Mountain Hiking

Discover the beauty of mountain trails and experience the tranquility of nature. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced hiker, our guide provides valuable information to help you plan your next adventure.

Featured Trails This Season

Spring brings fresh wildflowers and flowing streams to our local trails. The weather is perfect for moderate hikes, with temperatures ranging from 55-70°F. Popular trails this season include Bear Peak Loop, Royal Arch Trail, and Mount Sanitas.

Hiking Safety Tips

Always check weather conditions before heading out. Bring plenty of water (at least one liter per two hours of hiking), wear appropriate footwear, and let someone know your plans. Start early to avoid afternoon thunderstorms, especially during summer months.

Essential Gear Checklist

Pack the ten essentials: navigation tools, sun protection, insulation layers, illumination, first-aid supplies, fire starter, repair kit, nutrition, hydration, and emergency shelter. Even on short day hikes, being prepared can make the difference in an emergency situation.

Trail Etiquette

Respect nature and fellow hikers by following Leave No Trace principles. Stay on designated trails, pack out all trash, yield to uphill hikers, and keep dogs on leash. Be courteous and maintain a safe distance from wildlife.
